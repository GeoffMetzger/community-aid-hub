June 2026 – Illinois SUN Bucks (Summer EBT) Distribution Begins

The State of Illinois has launched its Summer 2026 SUN Bucks program, providing a one-time $120 grocery benefit per eligible school-aged child. Most families enrolled in SNAP, TANF, or income-eligible Medicaid will receive these benefits automatically on their current Link cards.

Source: Illinois Department of Human Services (IDHS)

Security Alert: Direct Protection Updates for Link Card Skimming

With federal replacement rules for stolen electronic benefits changing, IDHS urges all Chicago Link cardholders to monitor accounts closely. Recipients are strongly advised to change their PINs monthly and freeze their cards via the Link card portal when not actively shopping to prevent skimming theft.

Source: USDA Food and Nutrition Service & IDHS

📅 November 2025 – SNAP Benefits Fully Restored After Federal Shutdown

Following the end of the recent federal government shutdown, SNAP benefits have been fully reinstated nationwide, including in Illinois. EBT deposit systems are operating normally again, and recipients should begin seeing their regular benefit amounts on schedule.

If your benefits appear delayed or incorrect, check your Illinois Link account and contact Illinois DHS for assistance.

Source: USDA Food and Nutrition Service (FNS) & Illinois Department of Human Services (IDHS)
🥫 Community Food Support During Benefit Restoration

As SNAP systems return to normal, the Greater Chicago Food Depository and several partner sites across Bronzeville, Englewood, Auburn Gresham, and the South Suburbs are maintaining extended hours and added distributions to support families during the transition period.
Use the Find Food page to locate the nearest open pantry.

Source: Chicago Food Depository
